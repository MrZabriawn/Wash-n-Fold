Frequently Asked Questions

How does pricing work?

Wash & Fold pricing is based on weight after we weigh your laundry
at the shop. Pickup and delivery fees are flat based on distance.
We always confirm the total by text before starting.

Do I need to estimate the weight of my laundry?

No. Estimates are optional. We weigh everything ourselves.
Estimates just help us plan capacity.

What’s the turnaround time?

Standard service is typically next day. Same-day service is available
for orders placed by 9:00 AM, when capacity allows.

What detergent do you use? Can I request hypoallergenic?

We use quality commercial detergent. Hypoallergenic detergent or
customer-provided detergent is available—just note it in your order.

Can you wash bedding and larger items?

Yes. Comforters, blankets, and other bulky items are accepted.
Oversized items may affect turnaround time.

Do you offer pickup and delivery?

Yes. Pickup and delivery are available within our service area.
Fees are based on distance and confirmed by text after submission.

What if I’m outside the service area or need commercial service?

Submit a request and we’ll provide a custom quote.

Do you sell laundry bags?

Yes. We offer reusable laundry bags you keep for future orders:

  • Large (20-gallon) — fits roughly a standard week
    of clothes for one person
  • Extra Large (30-gallon) — fits a heavier week of clothes,
    multiple people, or clothing plus towels

Bag pricing: $10 (20-gallon) and $15 (30-gallon)
